ABOUT US

Begin a new adventure!

As someone who had lived overseas for most of my life, I finally had the opportunity to visit my homeland of Pakistan for an adventure! I wasn't sure what to expect, but I was amazed by the rich culture, delicious food, and breathtaking natural beauty hidden in the northern parts of the country. It was truly an eye-opening experience, and I was surprised to learn that many had yet to witness the stunning beauty that Pakistan has to offer. So, I teamed up with a group of local Pakistanis to share this beauty with the world, and Pak Travels was born!

We are dedicated to helping you discover the hidden gems of Pakistan, featuring awe-inspiring natural landscapes and impressive man-made wonders. Our trusted local tour guides will provide you with an in-depth look into the history and modern culture of different cities, ensuring you have an unforgettable journey through both urban and rural traditions. Join us on this adventure and start exploring the enchanting Northern Pakistan today!
